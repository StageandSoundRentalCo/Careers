Event Technician – Surrey

About the Role:

Stage and Sound Rental is seeking a skilled Event Technician to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will have experience in setting up, operating, and maintaining audio-visual equipment for various events.

Responsibilities:

  • Set up and operate sound systems, lighting, and staging equipment.
  • Perform pre-event checks to ensure all equipment is functioning correctly.
  • Troubleshoot technical issues during events.
  • Collaborate with event planners and clients to ensure their technical needs are met.
  • Maintain and repair equipment as needed.

Requirements:

  • Proven experience as an event technician or similar role.
  • Strong knowledge of audio-visual equipment and technologies.
  • Ability to work flexible hours, including evenings and weekends.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills.

Benefits:

  • Competitive salary.
  • Opportunities for professional development and training.
  • Friendly and supportive team environment.

Lighting Designer – Nottingham

Responsibilities:

Warehouse Manager – Liverpool

About the Role:

We are seeking an organised and efficient Warehouse Manager to oversee our inventory and logistics operations. The successful candidate will ensure that all equipment is stored safely and is ready for deployment to events.

Responsibilities:

  • Coordinate logistics for equipment delivery and collection.

  • Implement and oversee safety protocols in the warehouse.
  • Supervise warehouse staff and schedule shifts.

  • Strong organisational and multitasking skills.
  • Knowledge of inventory management systems.
  • Ability to lead and motivate a team.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills.

Benefits:

  • Competitive salary.

  • Opportunities for professional development.
  • Supportive and dynamic team environment.
